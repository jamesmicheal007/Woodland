Who We Are

Global Standards,
Indian Heart.

Woodland Group India is a premier provider of turnkey interior solutions, event production, and bespoke joinery. Headquartered in Mumbai, we serve clients across the nation—from the tech parks of Bangalore to the heritage sites of Rajasthan.

We specialize in transforming spaces into experiences. Whether it's a high-tech office in Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC) or a lavish wedding stage setup, our team combines precision engineering with the vibrant aesthetics that India loves.
