About Us

Woodland Groups

“Woodland Group is a premier production house specializing in bespoke event setups, exhibition stands, interior fit-outs, precision joinery, custom kiosks, and comprehensive branding solutions. We are committed to delivering exceptional craftsmanship, innovative concepts, and unparalleled service to our valued clients.”

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Woodland Groups began its journey in Dubai with humble beginnings—much like many other companies in the region. However, driven by determination, perseverance, and professional competence, the company steadily grew and expanded its footprint.

In 2018, Woodland Groups took a significant step forward by opening a branch in Sharjah, reinforcing its commitment to growth and operational excellence. Guided by a goal-oriented and focus-driven philosophy, the company continued its global expansion with the establishment of branches in London, United Kingdom, and Mumbai, India.

Today, Woodland Groups operates across the UAE, United Kingdom, and India, delivering comprehensive solutions in Event Set-Ups, Exhibition Stands, Interior Fit-Outs, and Digital Printing. We consistently work on a cost–quality–time matrix, ensuring optimal results across every project.

With a perfectly balanced blend of experience, innovation, commitment, and personalized service, Woodland Groups has evolved into one of the leading production contractors in the region, recognized for delivering professional, impactful, and exceptional results.

What We Do

We specialize in the designing and manufacturing of event setups, exhibition stands, interior fit-outs, kiosks, branding solutions, and all types of display systems for the BTL advertising industry. Our focus is on delivering innovative, high-quality concepts that consistently exceed our clients’ expectations.

We operate from a state-of-the-art 18,000 sq. ft. office and warehouse in Dubai and a 20,000 sq. ft. office and warehouse in Sharjah, fully equipped to handle fabrication and storage requirements—from small freestanding POS displays to large-scale event and exhibition setups.

With a complete in-house manufacturing facility, we have a strong advantage in producing premium-quality products at cost-effective prices, ensuring efficiency, consistency, and timely delivery.

Your “One-stop-shop” solution for Events, set-up, Exhibitions Stands, Interior Fit-out, Kiosks, Branding, Metal works, Paint booth, Acrylic fabrication and Events & Exhibitions furniture rental.

Vision

"Our goal is not merely to be the largest Events, Exhibitions, and Interior Fit-Out Production Company in the UAE, UK, and India—but to be the best. Through our passion and boundless creativity, we strive for excellence in everything we do."

As part of our global expansion, we have established branches in London, United Kingdom, and Mumbai, India, enabling us to offer the same high standards of service internationally.
